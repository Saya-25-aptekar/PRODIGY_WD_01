About Us

Welcome to Surya Restaurant, where tradition meets taste. Established with a vision to serve authentic Indian flavors, we bring you a dining experience that celebrates rich spices, fresh ingredients, and heartfelt hospitality.

At Surya, food is more than just a meal — it’s a celebration of culture and togetherness. Our chefs craft every dish with passion, ensuring every bite tells a story of India’s diverse culinary heritage. From classic vegetarian delicacies to mouth-watering non-vegetarian delights, we have something for everyone.

Whether you’re here for a casual family dinner, a romantic date, or a festive celebration, our warm ambiance, friendly staff, and top-quality service will make your visit truly memorable. Surya Restaurant is not just about food — it’s about creating unforgettable experiences.
